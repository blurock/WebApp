Essentually, registering as a new user is a two step process (step-by-step is given below). First, a valid email address, username and password are given. A valid email address is needed for the second 'authentication' step. An authentication email will be sent to the email and after clicking the link, the user will be registered. The user can then log in with the given username and password.
Register
Registering is a two stage process. In the first stage you enter a valid email and then a personal username and password (to be used for login). In the second authorization stage, an email will be sent to the email you gave and then you must click the link to register and validate your account.
To register, click the 'Create a new account' button on the right of the signin window and a new window will appear.
In the new window, you will be asked:
username:
This will be the user name to be used to sign in (if the username exists, you will be warned).
email:
A valid email must be given. This email will be used for the authentification process.
password
You will be asked for a password (and again to validate) that will used for login.
When you press 'Submit', then an email will be sent to the email address given. Within the email, there is a link which authenticates the account.
In the window that appears, you can enter your profile information and then login.
